About

Greetings!

I'm Tee Jay Zajac a multifaceted designer based in Charleston, South Carolina, spending my days in pursuit of good design. AIGA professional member since 2012. Bachelors of Science in Graphic Design Communication from Jefferson University (formerly Philadelphia University).

When not staring at a monitor agonizing over design I enjoy running, reading, DJing, and talking about music to absurd lengths.

Thank you for taking time out of your day to view my work!

Calculus Textbook

Lead designer on a Calculus textbook for Hawkes Learning. Worked closely with the author, lead editor, and all contributors. I created the cover, layout templates, table design, illustrations, and mathematically accurate graphs. I also handled all of the print production and correspondence with the printer.

Note: The cover Sculpture, Arabesque XXIX was done by Robert Longhurst, and Jameson Deichman was the contributing illustrator for the chapter openers.
  • Client: Hawkes Learning
  • Date: February 2015
  • Service: Publication Design & Illustration
  • More: Hawkes Learning: Calculus with Early Transcendentals
