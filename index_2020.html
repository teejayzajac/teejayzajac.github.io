Encore Galaxy Pavillion

Environmental Graphics

Designed in collaboration with Devin Meeks.

Created for the Galaxy Pavillion located on the Norwegian Cruise Lines’ Encore cruise ship. Client provided video of the space and rough blueprint.

Edges of the floor graphics were made intentionally organic to blend better with the existing floor finish. To help with installation, the large floor graphics were produced in panels and sent with installation instructions.

Client: Norwegian Cruise Lines
