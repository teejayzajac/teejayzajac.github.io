Encore Galaxy Pavillion

Environmental Graphics

Client: Norwegian Cruise Lines

Designed in collaboration with Devin Meeks.

Floor graphics created for the Galaxy Pavillion located on the Encore cruise ship. The client provided a quick video of the space, rough blueprint, and general measurements. They were looking to play up the galaxy theme while integrating with the existing attractions and branding.

Design

All sides of both designs were made intentionally organic (save for the video wall) to better hide the edge on the existing floor finish. Additionally an organic edge might increase the durability as it will be harder to cleanly lift an edge up.

Production & Installation

Additionally To help with installation, the large floor graphics were produced in panels and sent with installation instructions.

Roles: Graphic Design, Prepress
